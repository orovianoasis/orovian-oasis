Privacy Policy

Your information matters. This page explains how Orovian Oasis collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website.

✨

Our goal is simple: use your information to review your property request, contact you about your submission, and make the process as clear and respectful as possible.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a property form or contact us, we may collect information such as:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address.
  • The property address you submit.
  • Your ownership status, selling timeline, and optional property details.
  • Basic website usage data, such as pages viewed, button clicks, and form activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to review your property submission, follow up with you, schedule a walkthrough, prepare a possible cash offer, coordinate communication, and improve our website and process.

3. Calls, Texts, and Emails

By submitting your contact information, you agree that Orovian Oasis may contact you by phone, text message, or email about your property submission. Message and data rates may apply. You can ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

4. Analytics and Website Tools

We may use tools such as Google Analytics and Google Places to understand website activity, improve the form experience, and help sellers enter accurate property information. These tools may collect technical data such as browser type, device type, approximate location, and interactions with the website.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate our business, review your property, communicate with service providers, coordinate a transaction, comply with legal requirements, or protect our rights.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable efforts to protect submitted information. However, no website, email, text message, or online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party platforms such as social media pages, email, phone links, maps, analytics tools, or other services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
